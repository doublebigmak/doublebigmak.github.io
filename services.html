Services

Some professional services that I offer for business & corporate clients:

Forex/Commodity Hedging Strategy and Analysis

Your business may have significant exposure to foreign currencies that you might want to neutralize. We analyze your business' exposure and come up with appropriate hedging strategies to reduce potential risks.

Introductory consultations are free of charge.

Financial Statement/Portfolio Advisory

We apply standard investment analysis techniques to advise on the health and potential valuation of your business, through the lens of outside investment. We use this lens to recommend actions and areas of improvement which investors may look for. We can also determine if your liquid assets are being deployed optimally and if there are opportunities to improve returns on stagnant assets..

Introductory consultations are free of charge.

Data Science & Machine Learning Advisory

We advise on potential data science and machine learning opportunities that are available for your business, and if applicable, will devise and conduct a strategy for implementation. For growing businesses, we can also advise on creation of an in-house data analytics team.

Introductory consultations are free of charge.

Economic Advisory

We provide specific and relevant economic and demographic research related to your business, including potential risks and areas of opportunities for revenue growth and cost minimization. We can also help develop risk and production optimization analysis.

Introductory consultations are free of charge.
